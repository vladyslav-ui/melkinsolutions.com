Systems Engineering

HAGES Hardware & Software

A fully integrated, multi-modal robotic network engineered to shatter the endurance wall of high-consequence underground and confined space inspection.

Module 01: Aerial Platform

DJI Matrice 350 RTK

The agility asset. We utilize the industrial-grade M350 RTK platform, heavily modified for the harsh realities of subterranean flight. Operating without GPS, this platform relies entirely on SLAM integration to navigate confined, high-risk geometries.

Industrial Reliability

Equipped with the TB65 dual-battery system and an IP55 protection rating, the platform is engineered to withstand extreme dust and moisture common in mining and sewer environments.

Ingress Protection IP55
Max Takeoff Weight 9.2 kg
Flight Time (No Payload) Up to 55 min

Custom Propulsion Stack

To maximize flight time under full payload inside turbulent, confined airflows, the standard propulsion is upgraded to a specialized outrunner and carbon-fiber prop configuration.

Motor Architecture T-Motor MN5008 KV340
Propellers P15×5 Carbon Fiber
Flight Controller Cube Orange+
Module 02: Ground Persistence

goBILDA Overlander-T

The persistent backbone of the operation. This ruggedized, tracked mobile base transports the UAV deep into the hazard zone, acting as a mobile staging ground, launchpad, and the primary energy reserve for the entire hybrid system.

All-Terrain Mobility

Built for unpredictable environments. The flat skid-plate underbelly maximizes ground clearance, while the rubber-treaded tank tracks conform to rocky gravel, sewer inverts, and debris.

Traction System 48mm Badlands Tank-Tracks
Max Speed 2.3 mph
Payload Capacity 25 lbs (11.3 kg)

Energy Hub Integration

The Overlander-T’s spacious, enclosed chassis houses the heavy-duty energy systems required to not only drive the rover but repeatedly recharge the UAV mid-mission.

Primary Reserve 24V 30Ah LiFePO4 Dual-Pack
Power Conversion Integrated DC-AC Inverter
Base Weight (No Payload) 14.3 lbs
Module 03: Docking & Recharge

HEISHA DPad & D60

The core architectural breakthrough of HAGES. By integrating HEISHA’s industrial drone-in-a-box charging technology onto a mobile rover, the UAV can land, physically secure itself, recharge, and relaunch autonomously—completely shattering the standard drone endurance wall.

DPad C500 Rover Base

Mounted directly to the Overlander-T, the charging pad features an infrared sensor and sun-blocking shade to ensure perfect drone centering upon landing. Once landed, mechanical locking bars deploy to physically clamp the drone to the rover during transit.

Internal Logic 5-in-1 Precision PCB
Alignment System Infrared Centering & Limiter
Transit Security Automated Locking Bars

D60 Smart Landing Gear

Fitted to the Matrice 350, the D60 landing gear features gold-plated dual electrodes allowing up to 10A of current to pass through. This rapid energy transfer slashes the downtime between inspection sorties.

Weight 38 grams
Charge Current Up to 10A (Gold Electrodes)
Industrial Fatigue Rating ≥ 5,000 Landing Cycles
Module 04: Perception & Software

Emesent ST-X, Commander & Aura

The intelligence engine. HAGES utilizes the Emesent ecosystem to provide GPS-denied navigation, active omnidirectional collision avoidance, and the generation of survey-grade 3D digital twins.

Hovermap ST-X LiDAR

The primary sensing payload. The ST-X captures the environment to feed the SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) algorithm, allowing the drone to navigate blindly in the dark.

Sensing Array 32 Lasers, 3 Returns
Data Acquisition Up to 1.92M points/sec
Max Range 300 m

Commander & Autonomy

Through the Emesent Commander app, operators oversee the mission. In Autonomy mode, the operator simply taps waypoints on a live-streamed 3D map, and the ST-X plans a safe flight path via the "Shield" virtual safety bubble.

Virtual Safety Shield Collision Avoidance
Flight Modes Pilot Assist & Autonomous
Failsafe Protocol Smart RTH to Rally Point

Aura Post-Processing

While onboard processing provides a fast ($\sim$ 80s) localized point cloud in the field, Emesent Aura software utilizes global optimization to eliminate drift over long underground transits, producing survey-grade outputs.

Global Optimization Eliminates SLAM Drift
Cloud-to-Cloud Accuracy 6.7mm at 1-sigma
Processing Speed (15min) ~1,200s (Aura Engine)
