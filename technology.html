Deep Dive:
Hybrid Architecture

A four-layer operational stack combining cutting-edge SLAM perception, aerial agility, persistent ground mobility, and decision-ready data intelligence.

LAYER_01 PERCEPTION & AUTONOMY

Emesent Hovermap ST-X

The core intelligence of the system. Operating in GPS-denied environments, the Hovermap ST-X utilizes SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) to actively build a 3D map of its surroundings, enabling omnidirectional collision avoidance and autonomous path planning.

Sensors 32 Lasers, 3 Returns
Max Range 0.5 to 300 m
Cloud-to-Cloud Accuracy 6.7mm at 1-sigma
LAYER_02 AERIAL AGILITY

DJI Matrice 350 RTK

The agility asset. Built for high-consequence industrial deployments, the M350 acts as the aerial vehicle carrying the Hovermap payload into confined voids and complex geometries.

Max Flight Time Up to 55 minutes
Ingress Protection IP55
Sensing Six-Directional Sensing
LAYER_03 GROUND PERSISTENCE

Overlander-T Crawler

The solution to the "Endurance Wall." The tracked mobile base transports the drone deep into environments, bypassing UAV flight limits. The rover provides physical mounting for the HEISHA DPad auto-charging station, allowing the UAV to wirelessly recharge mid-mission without human intervention.

Payload Capacity 25 lbs (11.3 kg)
Max Speed & Runtime 2.3 mph / ~45 mins
Traction 48mm Badlands Tank-Tracks
LAYER_04 DATA PROCESSING

Emesent Aura

The intelligence engine. While onboard processing can generate quick, locally correct point clouds in ~80 seconds, Emesent Aura utilizes global optimization algorithms to produce high-resolution, survey-grade deliverables across the entire mission trajectory.

Onboard Processing Time ~80s (for 15 min mission)
Aura Processing Time ~1,200s (for 15 min mission)
Aura Accuracy Global SLAM Optimization
