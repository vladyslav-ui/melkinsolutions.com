The Bottleneck

For transit infrastructure, the ultimate hazard isn't toxic gas -- it's asset downtime. Mega-systems like Transport for London operate 402km of track with restricted maintenance windows from just 0200–0500 nightly. Manual crews simply cannot cover enough ground before morning service resumes.

The Linear Limitation

Long, linear scans are notoriously difficult for mobile-based LiDAR. Without varying structural features, standalone drones struggle to remove accumulated drift, creating a severe risk of SLAM slip resulting in scrambled data or drone crashes.
